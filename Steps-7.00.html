Reports and Dashboards created in Tableau 7

The following reports are created in Tableau dashboard-1

1. Top Level KPI's

a) Properties KPI - Total number of Properties loaded

b) Payment - Avg Monthly Payment for house

c) Section 8 Pay - Avg amount per month Section 8 will pay

d) Cash Flow - Difference between Payment and Section 8 payment


2. Top Left Sliders - I have used a calculation that considers (Down Payment / House Cost / Interest Rate) and calculates the monthly payment, therefore each person can put in their personal numbers and get a more accurate assessment of their particular situation.

a) Interest Rate - Projected Interest Rate

b) Down Payment - Projected Down Payment


3. Map

a) Map of current properties plotted by latitude and longitude. They are colour coordinated by Cash Flow.


4. Distribution Plot

a) Break down of Cash Flow by City and Bedroom


5. Table

a) Details of property along with Link to property
